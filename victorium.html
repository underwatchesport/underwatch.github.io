Victorium Cup

The Victorium Cup is a prestigious Overwatch tournament hosted by Underwatch, designed for players at the Masters rank and below. This competition aims to provide a thrilling and professional experience for both participants and spectators.

The tournament consists of two stages: a group stage followed by a single-elimination playoff. Matches are held twice a week, fostering a competitive yet accessible schedule for participants. Each season, we strive to improve based on community feedback, ensuring an ever-evolving and exciting competition.

Check out the official rulebook for details on participation, rules, and more!
